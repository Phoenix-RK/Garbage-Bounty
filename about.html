ABOUT US

GARBAGE BOUNTY


ADDING WORTH TO WASTE


Garbage Bounty is developed with the motto to provide a platform to help you get useful products in return of your wastes.

We act as a bridge in establishing connection between the user and the supplier, wherein we focus in the mutual benefit of the two parties.

While the user as well as the environment is benefitted by the products sold by the seller,
the seller is benefitted by the garbage he gets from the user,
which he uses as his raw material, with recycling being the biggest take away.
